NEWARK, Calif. - Lucid Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: LCID), known for its advanced electric vehicles, has announced the appointment of Nick Twork as Head of Global Communications. Twork, with over twenty years of experience in communications and marketing within the technology and automotive industries, is set to lead Lucid's international communications efforts.In his new role, Twork will manage a team responsible for product, corporate, financial, and internal communications, as well as media relations and social media. He will report directly to Lucid's CEO and CTO, Peter Rawlinson. Twork's extensive background includes roles at Ford (NYSE:F), GM, Porsche, Infiniti, and Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi, and he was a founding member at the energy storage company ONE (Our Next Energy).Peter Rawlinson expressed confidence in Twork's abilities to enhance the brand's profile and support Lucid's growth initiatives. Rawlinson highlighted Twork's deep understanding of the industry, leadership in emerging technology, and commitment to the company's goal of reducing carbon emissions in transportation.Twork's appointment comes as Lucid prepares to begin production of the Lucid Gravity, their first SUV, at the company's manufacturing facility in Arizona.
