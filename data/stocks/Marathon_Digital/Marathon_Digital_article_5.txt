The company's average operational hash rate grew by 2% month-over-month, reaching 26.3 EH/s, while its BTC holdings expanded to 18,536 BTC.The operational improvements at the Ellendale facility, which became fully operational at the start of July, contributed to a year-over-year doubling of the hash rate in June.
