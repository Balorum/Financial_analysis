Shares of Lucid Group (LCID) are trading higher Tuesday following news of additional funding from a major shareholder, Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund (PIF). Already holding nearly a 60% stake in the EV maker, the PIF has agreed to provide an extra $1.5 billion in funding.This financial boost comes as Lucid prepares to launch its newest electric vehicle model, the Lucid Gravity SUV.
