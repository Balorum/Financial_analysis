Netflix Inc. (NASDAQ:NFLX) Executive Chairman Reed Hastings has sold a significant portion of his company shares, according to the latest SEC filing. Hastings completed multiple transactions that resulted in the sale of shares totaling over $15 million. These sales were executed at varying prices, ranging from $664.7776 to $674.89.The transactions took place on July 1, 2024, and were reported in a Form 4 filing with the SEC on the following day. Hastings, who also serves as a director and officer of Netflix, carried out the sales under a pre-arranged Rule 10b5-1 trading plan, which was adopted on August 8, 2023. This type of trading plan allows corporate insiders to sell shares over a predetermined period of time, providing a defense against accusations of trading on nonpublic information.The Form 4 filing revealed a series of sales: 871 shares at an average price of $664.7776, 1892 shares at $665.7836, 1747 shares at $666.8378, 1206 shares at $667.7516, 1580 shares at $668.9746, 2326 shares at $670.0682, 3084 shares at $671.0602, 4028 shares at $672.1823, 3200 shares at $673.3247, and 2313 shares at $673.9656. Additionally, 279 shares were sold at a price of $674.89.While Hastings was selling shares, he also acquired 22,526 shares of Netflix common stock through the exercise of options at a price of $55.4871, totaling approximately $1.25 million.
